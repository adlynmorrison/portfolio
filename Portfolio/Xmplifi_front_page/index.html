How's it work?

Step One

Organization select beanded content they wish to promote though social networks.

Step Two

Content is automatically delivered to Xmplifi social media managers based on prioritization and pre-established criteria.

Step Three

Administrators select the content they want to promote, customize it if necessary, introduce the content, and then define the dates, times and social media channels to publish through.

Step Four

Xmplifi generates a unique URL for each piece of content for each promoter and for each social media channel so the campaign can be measured on a granular level.

Step Five

Xmplifi destributes the content, custom copy and unique URLs to promoters who then redistribute to their own social networks.

Step Six

Xmplifi monitors feedback and existing campaigns through Google Analytics and suggests optimizations based on content ans social channels that are delivering the best results.

Step Seven

real-time and post-campaign reporting enables administrators to make adjustments based on performance.
